Keyboard Shortcuts

Commander-style F-keys and macOS shortcuts

Function keys

KeyAction
F3Preview focused item
⌥F3Quick Look
F4Edit (text) or hex editor (binary)
F5Copy to other panel
F6Move to other panel
F7New folder
F8Delete (Trash)
⌥F8Permanent delete
F9Settings
F10Quit
